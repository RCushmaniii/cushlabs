**Business:** CushLabs.ai — AI Integration & Software Development Consulting. Serving US and Mexico.

**Ideal Client Profile:**

**What I'm Looking For on LinkedIn:**
1. Companies posting about pain points CushLabs solves (manual processes, support volume, onboarding)
2. Decision makers at companies in the 50-500 employee range
3. Job postings for "AI consultant", "automation engineer", "chatbot developer" — signals the company needs this capability
4. People posting about digital transformation, AI strategy, or struggling with operational efficiency
5. Potential B2B partnerships with agencies, consultancies, or tech companies that could refer work

**Search Keywords:** "AI strategy", "digital transformation", "automation", "chatbot", "workflow automation", "AI consulting", "operational efficiency", "customer service automation"

**Tone for any outreach drafts:** Professional but warm, consultative not salesy. Lead with value and insight, not pitches. Bilingual capability is a differentiator for LATAM-facing companies.

## Sample Prompts for the Chrome Extension

```

in professional services or SaaS. Find 10 profiles that look like good prospects
for CushLabs and tell me why each one is a fit.
```

### Job Posting Signals
```
Search LinkedIn Jobs for "AI consultant", "chatbot developer", or "automation engineer"
posted in the last 7 days by companies with 50-500 employees. These companies need
AI capability — compile a list with company name, role posted, and the hiring manager
if visible.
```

### Partnership Search
```
Find digital agencies or IT consultancies in the US that serve mid-market clients
and don't have AI/automation in their service offerings. These are potential referral
partners for CushLabs. List 10 with company name, key contact, and why they'd benefit
from partnering.
```
