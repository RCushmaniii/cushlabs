## Search #3 Methodology Notes

This query ("customer service automation for small business") is **vendor-intent**, not **buyer-intent**. Google returns tool comparison articles and vendor landing pages. Better approaches for finding CS automation buyers:

- **Reddit r/smallbusiness** — founders naming their companies and describing CS pain
- **Shopify partner directories** — mid-size DTC brands without automation
- **Viral DTC brands** — TIME Best Invention, Product Hunt launches, TikTok viral products create sudden CS volume spikes
- **Job boards filtered to "Head of CX" or "CS Manager"** at companies with < 200 employees — signals they're building the function from scratch
